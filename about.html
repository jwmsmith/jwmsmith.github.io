About

Media bias is all the rave to talk about. Allegations of Fake News or lack of perspective is tiring and can leave one feeling demotivated to keep up with the news. The key is not to trust a single source, but to see all at once. This way one can see through the storm and get a birds eye view of different narratives. This is what Political News Spectrum aims to provide. We give you windows into news sites across the political spectrum so you can compare different narratives at the same time.
